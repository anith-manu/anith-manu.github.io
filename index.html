Research

PhD Thesis: Techniques For Subtle Mid-Air Gestural Interaction Using mmWave Radar

Anith Manu Ravindran

My recently published PhD thesis explores techniques for mid-air gestural interaction using miniature radar. It addresses challenges such as the Midas Touch problem and investigates the use of signal processing and deep learning for robust gesture recognition. The research focuses on enabling real-time interaction through subtle, intentional hand gestures, demonstrating how radar-based sensing can support more effortless and socially acceptable forms of input.
